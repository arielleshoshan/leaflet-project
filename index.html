Popular places to visit in Japan

Why Japan?

My name is Arielle Shoshan. I have always wanted to travel to Japan. I have worked in ramen bars, studied the Japanese culture, and have been learning the language. I have created this map using Leaflet to show the locations that I would love to visit.

The locations I chose are notable for some of the best tourist attractions in Japan. Other locations are for anime, manga, and video game enjoyers. Several historical locations from parks, temples, and torii gates are marked as well. There are also two locations famous for ramen. If I were going to Japan, I would visit each marked location happily.

Please interact and explore the map above by clicking on the markers or zooming in and out.
